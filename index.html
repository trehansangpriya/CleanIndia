About The App

“Gamified Swachh Bharat App - Clean India” focuses on creating a win-win situation for the Initiative by Govt. of India and also the citizens of the Nation.

A Mobile Application that enables a citizen to post a civic related issue which is then forwarded to the city corporation and also spread awareness about public and personal hygiene, in return the citizens receive points which can be redeemed at any local store/vendor.

Features
Daily Bonus
The Daily Bonus Feature lets you snap a picture of the user taking out the trash or segregating Wet and Dry waste or any hygienic activity, in their house or their surroundings and upload it to our servers using the Clean India App.

Our Machine Learning Model Recognizes the Image and award Points to the User which can be later Redeemed.
Your Feed
Your Feed Section is a Social Media Platform for all the users where they can engage with the Online Community of the App, Like, Share and Comment on each others’ updates.

And also Share their Achievements, Prizes, Points earned in the App, along with beneficial Posts that are good for the Community can also be posted. The content will be monitored by a strict AI Model.
Redeem Points
Redeem Section is used to redeem points that a user gains/earns in the App at the local Stores/Vendors in his/her city.

The Points will have a distinctive conversion System which will be followed by the User and the Local Vendors as well. The proposed Conversion rate for now is 100 Pts = ₹10
Report an Issue
Report Section is used by the users to report any activity to the Authorities via the App, by uploading a photo of the issue and along with it a message describing the issue.

The Location of the user and issue is taken byt he App using the Geolocation API and is forwarded to the Authorities using End to End Encryption, keeping the user’s information safe at all times. The Authoroties can then take action on the issue raised by the user.
